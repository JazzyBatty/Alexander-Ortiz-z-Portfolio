About Me

The Beginning

I started to take drawing seriously around 4th grade. Ever since then, I strived to improve my art as much as I can every day. Wheather it be a fully illustrated picture or just a rough sketches of various ideas in my head, I always strive to improve.

My Life Long Dream

My biggest goal in life is to work in the video game industry and create a video game of my very own. I love to create and develop stories so they could be told in the form of video games. I also love to design characters that I hope one day I could put into a video game too.
